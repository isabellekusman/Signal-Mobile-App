# Privacy Policy for Signal

**Effective Date:** February 20, 2026

Signal ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Signal Mobile Application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
- **Account Data:** Email address and password when you create an account.
- **Profile Data:** Name, zodiac sign, relationship standards, boundaries, and attachment styles.
- **Connection Data:** Information you log about your relationships, including names, icons, and signal observations.
- **Communication Log Data:** Text or images you upload for AI "Decoding" and analysis.

### B. Automatically Collected Data
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data:** Frequency of App use, features accessed, and subscription status.
- **Push Notification Tokens:** We collect device tokens to send you relationship insights and reminders.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **To Provide the Service:** Using AI to analyze your relationship signals and provide insights.
- **To Improve the App:** Developing new features and optimizing AI response accuracy.
- **Personalization:** Tailoring astrological and behavioral insights to your profile.
- **Safety & Security:** Detecting and preventing abuse or prompt injection attacks.
- **Communication:** Sending you relevant updates and notifications.

## 3. Data Processing & AI

Signal utilizes **Google Gemini AI** to process and analyze the text and images you provide. 
- Your relationship data is sent to AI models for processing. 
- **Disclaimer:** AI-generated content is for reflection only and does not constitute clinical psychological advice or factual truth.

## 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with service providers only as necessary:
- **Supabase:** For database hosting, authentication, and secure data persistence.
- **Google Cloud (Gemini):** For AI processing.
- **RevenueCat:** For managing App Store subscriptions and payments.
- **Sentry:** For error logging and performance monitoring.

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
- **Access & Portability:** You can export your data at any time via the "Me" screen in the App.
- **Deletion:** You can permanently delete your account and all associated data via the App settings.
- **Correction:** You can update your profile information in real-time.

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:
- **Encryption at Rest:** Your data is stored securely in Supabase.
- **Secure Proxy:** AI API keys are never stored on your device.
- **Row Level Security:** Only you can access your relationship data.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@getsignal.app
